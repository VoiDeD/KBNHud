I noticed that KNHUD, my favorite Team fortress 2 HUD was missing some stuff. I figured that it couldn't hurt to fix that with some help from BroselHUD, a similar HUD. However, the new plan for the HUD is to make it it's own thing, and not a copycat of KNHUD anymore. We can thanks Sinders and his eveHUD for most of the materials we needed to reach our goal.

I started this HUD because I like to be able to use certain things without having to rely on chance. Take the Pomson or the Cow mangler. I know the mangler has 5 shots, but if I switch weapons, i lose count of how many I have.

Now, chippy has rebuilt KNHUD into KNHUD 2.0, but I am personally dissatisfied with the new design, and I believe his redesign also was meant to allow for greater differentiation between hid HUD and this one, so I am keeping this HUD alive as a fix and slight restyling of the OLD KNHUD.
